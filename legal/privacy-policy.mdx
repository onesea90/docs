3. **To send administrative emails and other communications** - We may send you service, technical, and other administrative emails, messages, and other types of communications
4. **To provide Customer support** - If you send us a request, we respond to your request or help your issues
5. **Security purposes** - To investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, and situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person
6. **In accordance with or as may be required by law**
